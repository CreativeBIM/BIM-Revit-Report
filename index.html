Regulatory Landscape

Understanding the regulatory environment for BIM and Revit implementation is crucial for ensuring compliance and maximizing project efficiency. Below is an overview of key regulations in the selected countries:

Country Regulation
Belgium Mandatory for public projects since 2019.
USA Regulations vary by state, with increasing adoption in federal projects.
Germany Mandatory for public infrastructure projects since 2020.
France Encouraged for public projects; mandatory by 2022 for certain types.
UK Mandatory for all public sector construction projects since 2016.
Italy Gradually mandatory for public projects, starting with larger contracts.
India Not mandatory, but increasingly used in large-scale projects.
UAE Mandated for all major construction projects in Dubai since 2014.
Netherlands Strongly encouraged by the government for public works.
Mexico No national mandate, but increasingly adopted by leading firms.
Colombia No official mandate, but adoption is growing in large infrastructure projects.
Austria Encouraged in public projects, with full adoption expected by 2025.
Spain Mandatory for public projects by 2022.
